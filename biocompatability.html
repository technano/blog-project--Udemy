Biocompatability Testing of Biological Materials and Implants Per ISO 10993

Ref: FDA Biocompatability Guidance Document

Ref: ISO10993

How to determine the type of Biocompatability tests required:


    Identify Nature of contact:


Is the Device:

  1. Surface Contacting Device

    • Intact Skin
    • Mucosal Membrane
    • breached or Compromised Skin Surface

  2. External Communicating Device

    • Contacting Blood Path Indirectly
    • Contacting Tissue+/bone/ dentin
    • Contacting Circulating blood

  3. Implant

    • Contacting Tissue/Bone
    • Blood

    Identify Contact Duration :

    • A – limited-(<24 h)
    • B – prolonged-(>24 h to 30 d)
    • C – permanent-(> 30 d)

    Type of Tests:

    • Cytotoxicity
    • Sensitization
    • Irritation or Intracutaneous Reactivity
    • Acute Systemic Toxicity
    • Material-Mediated Pyrogenicity
    • Subacute/Subchronic Toxicity
    • Implantation
    • Genotoxicity
    • Chronic Toxicity
    • Hemocompatability
    • Carcinogenicity
